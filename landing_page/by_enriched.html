Enriched Extra Curricular

The Bnos Yaakov philosophy is that learning and personal growth do not end with the dismissal bell. Through our many exciting extracurricular activities, chesed initiatives and special community projects, every student, regardless of academic ability, can pursue her passion and shine in her own way. Beyond providing an outlet for creativity and increasing self- esteem, extracurricular pursuits also help students develop leadership and problem-solving skills and teach the value of cooperation and negotiation. Further, they provide opportunities for students to cultivate and develop “real- world” talents and abilities that can be directed towards meaningful post-high school pursuits.
