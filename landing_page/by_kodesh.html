Limudei Kodesh

Our Limudei Kodesh curriculum is designed to illuminate Torah’s timeless beauty and its relevance to every aspect of the girls’ daily lives. Students are inspired to carry on their rich mesorah with pride. Through text-based, open class discussions and creative independent work, students are trained in rigorous thought and analysis. Refinement of midos and character development are strongly emphasized, as is in-depth understanding of how mitzvah performance strengthens a Yid’s connection to the Ribbono Shel Olam. Working in partnership with the girls’ families, we strive to inculcate sensitivity towards tznius in dress and deportment and to provide halachic and hashkafic tools for living Torah lives in an increasingly materialistic and technologically driven world.

Chumash

Classes will be conducted with lectures, discussions and group work and will cover all of Chumash.

Parshas Hashavua / Haftorah

The younger classes will encompass the Parsha and focus on key Rashi’s. Girls will work in groups to develop teacher guided textual skills. Older classes will also learn the Haftorah and its connection to the Parshas Hashavua. Learning the Haftorah will be an effective means of gaining a broader knowledge of all Neviyim.

Navi

The girls will learn Milachim bais, Sefer Yonah, Yirmiyahu, Yeshayahu, Trey Asar, Yechezkel, Neviyim Rishonim

Kesuvim

Tehillim, Mishlei, Megilas Esther, Mishlei, Megilas Rus, Shir Hashirim

Tehillim/Tefilah/Shirah

Bnos Yaakov will stress understanding of Milim, proper pronunciation, and an inspiration of and attachment to tefilah. Girls will learn the origin and power of Shirah. Music lessons and songs will be woven throughout the teaching of Tehillim.

History

Brias Haolam until Tekufas HaGeonim Medieval Jewish History Torah Personalities Churban Europa (Holocaust studies) and Nashim B’Tnach The curriculum will include artifacts, pictures, interviews, period musical compositions, small group work, analyzing documents, preparing timelines and visits.

Halacha

• Hilchos Shabbos and Yom Tov • Hilchos Tefilah • Hilchos Kibud Av Veim, Hilchos Derech Eretz • Hilchos Lashon Hara, Hilchos Emes V’cheses • Hilchos Kashrus, Brachos

Machshava

Chovos Halvanos, Mesilas Yesharim, Michtav M’Eliyahu Contemporary Jewish issues, principles of the Jewish woman and Jewish home

Ivrit

Knowledge and skills of Lashon Hakodesh and Hebrew Girls will learn to be fluent in Ivrit both orally and textually, which will aid them in learning all their other subjects easily. All the literature used in the curriculum will also serve as means of imparting and inculcating vpeav and personal growth.

Yamim Tovim

Dinim, History and Machshava A week before every Yom Tov, teachers in all grades teach a unit on the upcoming Yom Tov, deepening the girls’ knowledge and appreciation of the Yom Tov. All the grades learn and create projects on the same topic together, creating an atmosphere of achdus, friendship and simcha throughout the school!

Chessed

Girls are required to do chessed during school time or on Sundays. Bnos Yaakov will hire a chessed coordinator who successfully matches each student to a chessed organization or program they feel comfortable with: special needs children, the elderly, tutoring, Packing for Bikur Cholim or Purim, children in hospitals, etc. Students needed at home can fulfill their chessed requirements through ‘in house’ chessed.
